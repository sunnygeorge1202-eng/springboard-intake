Springboard Talent
✦
A different kind of career conversation.
Springboard Talent is a small, invitation-based programme run by a group of senior professionals — a coach, an assessor, a talent connector, and a career strategist. We work with a limited number of people at a time, and we work closely.

We don't send your CV to a hundred companies. We help you figure out what's next, make sure you're ready for it, and then open the right doors.
1
This intake form — 10 minutes
Tells us who you are and where you're headed. Some questions let you respond by voice — speak naturally, no script needed.
2
A discovery call — 45 minutes
One of our team will call you within 48 hours. This is a real conversation, not a screening. We want to understand your full story.
3
Coaching and assessment
Depending on your goals, you'll work with our coach and assessor to sharpen how you show up — on paper and in person.
4
Introductions that matter
When you're ready, we make warm introductions to companies and leaders who are genuinely right for you. Not mass applications.
This is completely free for you. We do not charge candidates for anything — ever. Your information is kept confidential and shared only with the people directly involved in your journey.
